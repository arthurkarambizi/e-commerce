About us

Who We Are

AChair is an online marketplace for design lovers to buy furniture and art. We are a growing marketplace dedicated to home decorating and design.

What We do

in Achair all of our products are manufactured by local artists from all over the world.

Once an item sells, AChair orchestrates shipping the item from the seller to the buyer. You can pick your purchase up in person if you like, but we're here to do the heavy lifting if you need it.

When shoppers receive their AChair purchase, they have two days to let us know if they want to return it
